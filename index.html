Koala Living Style

Koalas are native to Australia and have a unique living style. Here are some interesting facts:

  • Koalas spend most of their time in eucalyptus trees, which are their primary habitat.
  • They have a slow metabolic rate and sleep for up to 20 hours a day.
  • Koalas have sharp claws and strong limbs that help them climb and grip tree branches.
  • They are well-adapted to their arboreal lifestyle and have a keen sense of balance.
